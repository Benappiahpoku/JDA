About Us

Jerry Donkor & Associates

Jerry Donkor & Associates was established to provide bespoke project management services to clients across Africa. The core values of the firm to be client oriented in providing peace of mind as well as value for money in managing projects within budget and on time. We are project managers with speciality in Hospitality (Hotels, Hospitals, Malls, Luxury Villas etc). We specilize in managing the extent and limits of Client requirements (cost, time, and quality), Operators requirements (quality and time), and Funding requirements (time,cost,risks, bonds, and securities). We will be pleased to share our expertise with you.

Services

Preliminary Project Management Services

This includes the review of clients initial documents, the target program envelope according to financial constraints. It also includes the establishment of a strategic execution plan.

General Project Management

This involves the reviewing of the project program and the design brief issued by the operator or the client. It also covers the monitoring and controlling of the various scopes of services for the clients.

Contractors's Design & Validation

This involves the review and approval of drawings, Manufactures manuals, Certificate of work test and as-built data.

Resident Technical Supervision

This involves the review of Contractor's Quality control plan as well as the day to day Technical supervision of the construction works.

Budget & Cost Control

This comprises of the certifying of interim payments and final payments certificate and the advisory services to the client in respect of potential unplanned expenditure as it becomes evident.

Value Engineering Process

This service is required when the Cost Estimation prepared by the Project Quantity surveyor exceeds the Project Budget and Durations Scheduled in consideration of the funding and business requirements such as the Return on Investment (ROI), etc.

Brand Guideline Development

This service involves the creation of a Brand Guideline Document in compliance with International Brand Standards to allow room for future acquisitions, mergers, management, and franchise agreement with existing Brands as well as meet the Marketing and Operational Standards focused on Revenue generation.

Document Control Services

This involves the paperless codification to enable digital filing systems for easy retrieval as well as the management of version control in a project environment.

Construction Management

This involves the managment of the construction phase of the project when this is required by the client.
